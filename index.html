Hardcore Classes Enhanced

41 lore-based character archetypes for WoW Classic Hardcore. Pick an enhanced class and the addon tracks your challenge rules, gear restrictions, quest milestones, and talent requirements as you level.

v0.8.6 WoW Classic Era 41 Enhanced Classes

Real-Time Tracking

The addon monitors equipment, talents, quests, challenges, and more in real time. Violations are flagged instantly with alerts.

Requirements Panel

A persistent, draggable checklist: green checkmarks for met rules, red crosses for violations, grey for level-locked requirements. Access via /cce panel.

Auto-Detection

Your enhanced class is determined by race, gender, and class. The addon detects it on login. Use /cce pick to choose manually.

Installation

Drop the HardcoreClassesEnhanced folder into your Interface/AddOns directory. Works with Classic Era (Interface 11507).
